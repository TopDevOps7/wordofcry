BREEDING

Cryptoids can breed with each other to produce superior offspring with similar traits.

The maximum number of breeds is capped at seven (7) per cryptoid. A combination of CAC and CGC will be required to breed Cryptoids when paired with each other.

Cryptoids each have different skills that can be used to destroy your opponent!
